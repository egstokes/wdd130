Above is the map of Galdin. The blue icons represent the human Dutchy of Kent, the orange icons show the the Domain of the Elves (Tandia), and the green icons are all in the regions of Gravier, the Dwarf Kingdom.

The above map is that of Fort St. Denn, a key outpost on the frontier of the lands of humanity and a vital defense point against the ever present threats that could head north from the lands of Desolation. Thanks to this fort, the Ducthy of Kent has been able to ward of many invasions from both orc and hobgoblin raiding parties.
